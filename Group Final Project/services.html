Stuffed Mushrooms

Time

Prep Time: 25 mins Cook Time: 20 mins Total Time: 45 mins
Servings: 12 Yield: 12 stuffed mushrooms

Ingredients

  • 12 whole fresh mushrooms
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 (8 ounce) package cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • ¼ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cayenne pepper

Instructions

Step 1
Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray a baking sheet with cooking spray.

Step 2
Clean mushrooms with a damp paper towel; carefully break off stems. Chop stems extremely fine, discarding the tough end of stems.

Step 3
Heat oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add garlic and chopped mushroom stems; fry until any moisture has evaporated, taking care not to burn garlic. Set aside to cool.

Step 4
Stir in cream cheese, Parmesan cheese, black pepper, onion powder, and cayenne. The mixture will be very thick.

Step 5
Use a teaspoon to fill each mushroom cap with a generous amount of stuffing. Arrange mushroom caps on the prepared cookie sheet.

Step 6
Bake in the preheated oven until the mushrooms are piping hot, about 20 minutes.

Step 7
Serve and enjoy!

Nashville Hot Mozzarella Sticks

Time

Prep Time: 5 mins Cook Time: 25 mins Total Time: 1hr
Freeze Time: 30 mins Servings: 4

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) block mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 tablespoon kosher salt, divided
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper, divided
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • 2 teaspoons hot sauce
  • 1 1/3 cups seasoned breadcrumbs
  • 2/3 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 4 tablespoons paprika, divided
  • 2 cups vegetable oil for frying, or more as needed
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 3 tablespoons cayenne pepper
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • ranch dressing for dipping

Instructions

Step 1
Cut mozzarella cheese into 8 “plank”-shaped pieces, about 1/2-inch thick.

Step 2
Whisk flour in a shallow dish with 1 teaspoon salt and 1/2 teaspoon pepper. In a second shallow dish, add beaten egg and whisk in hot sauce. In a third shallow dish, stir together breadcrumbs and 1 teaspoon paprika. Set a wire rack inside a rimmed baking sheet.

Step 3
Coat each piece of cheese in flour and shake off excess. Coat evenly with egg mixture and then place in the breadcrumbs. Coat evenly with breadcrumbs.

Step 4
Return cheese to the egg and back into breadcrumbs, lightly pressing to adhere breadcrumbs. Place breaded cheese on the prepared wire rack. Repeat breading process with remaining cheese pieces. Place in freezer for 30 minutes.

Step 5
Meanwhile, pour oil into a deep saucepan to a depth of 3 inches, and heat oil to 365 to 375 degrees F (185 to 190 degrees C). Combine remaining salt, pepper, and paprika with garlic powder, cayenne, and brown sugar in a heatproof bowl; stir until well blended.

Step 6
Working a few at a time, carefully lower cheese pieces into hot oil. Fry about 1 minute, turning occasionally, until golden brown and crisp. Drain on wire rack.

Step 7
Carefully pour 1 1/4 cups hot oil into the bowl with cayenne mixture and stir until well combined. Coat each cheese stick lightly in seasoned oil and return to wire rack to allow excess to drip off.

Step 8
Serve with ranch dressing for dipping.

Caprese Garlic Bread

Time

Prep Time: 15 mins Cook Time: 10 mins Total Time: 25 mins
Servings: 8

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) loaf French bread
  • olive oil spray
  • 4 tablespoons salted butter, softened
  • 5 cloves garlic, pressed
  • 12 ounces fresh mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • 4 small tomatoes, sliced, or more as needed
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/3 cup chopped fresh basil
  • drizzle of balsamic glaze

Instructions

Step 1
Preheat the oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C), and line a baking sheet with parchment paper. Cut bread in half lengthwise, lightly spray with olive oil, and place on the baking sheet, cut side up.

Step 2
In a small bowl, combine butter and garlic; spread evenly on the bread halves, and top with mozzarella slices.

Step 3
Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, and bread edges are starting to brown, 8 to 11 minutes.

Step 4
Remove from the oven, place tomato slices on top of the cheese, season with salt and pepper, and sprinkle with basil. Drizzle with balsamic glaze, cut into slices, and serve.
